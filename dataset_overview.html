TTGBench Dataset Overview

TTGBench provides graph datasets enriched with textual attributes, timestamped links, and real-time labels, spanning key temporal graph learning tasks, multiple scales, and diverse domains.

Comprehensive task coverage: We include both the future link prediction and the dynamic node classification tasks
, with the latter accommodating both multi-class and multi-label classification settings.

Rich textual attributes: We introduce temporal graph datasets in which both nodes and edges are associated with textual attributes.

Diverse scale and more emerging edges: TTGBench datasets span small, medium and large scales
, and feature a higher proportion of novel edges (i.e., with few recurring edges).

Rich domains: TTGBench datasets come from diverse fine-grained domains, including culinary recipe feedback, movie reviews, book reading records, beer rating data and online shopping interactions.
